# Results

## Executive Summary
- Total players: **3,024**
- Purchase transactions: **2,887**
- Total revenue: **€296,259.31**
- Purchaser conversion: **95.47%**

## Key Findings
1. Android is the dominant platform (~59%).
2. India has the largest player population.
3. Minnows represent most users.
4. Whales contribute disproportionately to revenue.
5. Debit Card is the highest revenue payment method.
6. Engagement metrics have weak correlation with spending.
